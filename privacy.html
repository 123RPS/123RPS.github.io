Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

Overview

123RPS respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected and how it is used in connection with the app.

Information We May Collect

  • Usernames you choose for public leaderboard participation
  • Gameplay statistics such as wins, losses, and draws
  • Basic app usage or technical information needed to operate the service

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Provide gameplay and leaderboard functionality
  • Maintain app performance and reliability
  • Support users and resolve technical issues

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is not shared with third parties except where necessary to operate, maintain, or improve the app, or where required by law.

Public Information

If you choose a public username for leaderboard participation, that username and related game statistics may be visible to other users.

Data Retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app’s features, maintain records, or comply with legal obligations.

Children’s Privacy

123RPS is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: info@123rps.com
